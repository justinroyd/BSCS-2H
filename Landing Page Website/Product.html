Best Foods in Batangas

Kapeng Barako

Kapeng Barako is a type of coffee grown in the Philippines, known for its strong and bold flavor.

Lomi

Batangas Lomi is a type of Filipino noodle soup dish originating from the Batangas province in the Philippines.

Atcharang

Atcharang Batangas is a Filipino dish that consists of pickled vegetables, usually carrots, green papaya, and ginger, mixed with grated coconut, shrimp paste, and spices.

Honey

Homemade honey is a sweet, amber-colored liquid made by bees using nectar from flowers. It is made by collecting nectar from flowers, which is then brought back to the bee hive and processed by the bees.

Balisong

A balisong, also known as a butterfly knife, is a type of folding knife with two handles that rotate around the tang of the blade. The blade is typically hidden between the handles when the knife is closed, and can be quickly deployed by flipping the handles open. Balisongs originated in the Philippines and were traditionally used for everyday tasks such as cutting food and opening cans.

Panutsa

Panutsa is a traditional Filipino sweet made from sugar, glutinous rice and peanuts. It is a sticky, caramelized candy that is often cut into small squares and wrapped in banana leaves or cellophane. The peanuts are first roasted then ground into a paste and mixed with sugar syrup and glutinous rice flour.
